Crystals Collector

You will be given a random Number Goal at the start of the game. There are five crystals below, each containing a different number. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total points. You win the game by matching your total points to the Number Goal. You will lose the game if your total points goes above the Number Goal. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Every time the game starts, values of each crystal change. Good luck!
